External and Internal Features

Fishes have specialized body parts that help them swim, breathe, and survive underwater.

  • Gills – For breathing underwater.
  • Scales – Protect the body from injury and infection.
  • Fins – Aid in movement, balance, and direction.
  • Lateral line – Detects vibrations and movement in water.
