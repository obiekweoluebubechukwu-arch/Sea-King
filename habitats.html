Where Do Fishes Live?

Fishes inhabit various environments around the world — from shallow rivers to deep ocean trenches.

  • Freshwater – Lakes, rivers, and ponds (e.g., Catfish, Tilapia).
  • Saltwater – Oceans and seas (e.g., Tuna, Clownfish).
  • Brackish – Mixture of salt and fresh water (e.g., Mullet, Goby).
