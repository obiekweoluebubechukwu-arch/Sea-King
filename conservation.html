Protecting Our Aquatic Life

Fish populations face threats from overfishing, pollution, and climate change. Conservation efforts focus on maintaining healthy aquatic ecosystems.

  • Promoting sustainable fishing.
  • Reducing plastic and chemical pollution.
  • Restoring coral reefs and mangroves.
  • Raising awareness about endangered species.
